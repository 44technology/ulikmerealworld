## Introduction

### What is Ulikme?

Ulikme is a platform that connects people through local activities, events, and classes. As an entrepreneur, you can:

- **Create Classes** - Share your expertise through structured learning experiences
- **Create Activities** - Organize small, intimate gatherings (â‰¤10 people)
- **Create Events** - Host larger gatherings (>10 people) with ticketing and enhanced features

### Who Can Create?

- **Instructors** - Teachers, coaches, mentors who want to monetize their expertise
- **Venue Owners** - Restaurants, cafes, parks, event spaces who want to host activities
- **Entrepreneurs** - Anyone with knowledge or experience to share
- **Content Creators** - People who want to build a community around their interests

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Download the Ulikme mobile app or visit the web portal
2. Sign up using:
   - Phone number + OTP verification
   - Google Sign-In
   - Apple Sign-In
3. Complete your profile:
   - Add your name, bio, and interests
   - Upload photos
   - Verify your account with a selfie

### Step 2: Choose Your Role

**For Instructors:**
- Select "Instructor" role during registration
- Provide professional credentials (optional but recommended)
- Set up your instructor profile

**For Venue Owners:**
- Select "Venue" role during registration
- Provide business information
- Upload business license (for verification)

**For Entrepreneurs:**
- You can create activities/events as a regular user
- Upgrade to instructor/venue role for advanced features

## Creating an Event

- **Ticketing** - Sell tickets, manage capacity
- **Agenda** - Structured schedule
- **Check-in** - QR code check-in system
- **Enhanced Visibility** - Better promotion

### When to Use Event Mode

**Use Events When:**
- Expecting >10 attendees
- Need ticket management
- Want structured agenda
- Require check-in system
- Need professional organization

**Use Activities When:**
- â‰¤10 attendees
- Casual, flexible gathering
- No need for ticketing
- Intimate social experience

### Writing Descriptions

**Structure:**
1. **Hook** - Grab attention in first sentence
2. **What** - What will happen
3. **Who** - Who should attend
4. **When/Where** - Specific details
5. **What to Bring** - If applicable

**Example - Good Description:**
```
Join us for an exciting tennis match at Flamingo Park! Whether you're a 
beginner or advanced player, this is a great opportunity to meet fellow tennis 
enthusiasts and improve your game. We'll play doubles matches and rotate 
partners so everyone gets to play. Bring your racket and water bottle. 
All skill levels welcome!
```

**Example - Bad Description:**
```
Tennis match. Come play.
```

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Can I create both classes and activities?**
A: Yes! You can create both. Classes are for teaching, activities are for social gatherings.

**Q: What's the difference between Activity and Event?**
A: Activities are for â‰¤10 people (intimate, flexible). Events are for >10 people (ticketing, agenda, check-in).

**Q: How do I get paid for paid classes/activities?**
A: Payments are processed through the platform. You receive payment minus 4% processing fee. Payouts are typically weekly.

**Q: Can I edit my class/activity after creating it?**
A: Yes, you can edit most details. Some changes (like price increases) may require approval.

**Q: What happens if no one enrolls/joins?**
A: You can cancel or reschedule. For paid classes, you'll get a full refund if cancelled before start date.

### Class-Specific Questions

**Q: How do I set up online classes?**
A: Select "Online" type, choose meeting platform (Zoom, Teams, etc.), and provide meeting link.

**Q: Can I offer free trials?**
A: Yes, create a free class as a trial, then offer paid advanced classes.

**Q: What if a student wants a refund?**
A: Students can request refunds within 24 hours of enrollment. You'll be notified to process.

**Q: How many students should I accept?**
A: Depends on your teaching style. 10-20 is optimal for engagement. More for lectures, fewer for hands-on.

**Q: Can I create recurring classes?**
A: Yes, select "Weekly" or "Custom" frequency and set up multiple sessions.

### Activity/Event-Specific Questions

**Q: When should I use Event mode vs Activity mode?**
A: Use Event mode for >10 people, when you need ticketing, or want structured agenda. Use Activity for smaller, casual gatherings.

**Q: Can I change an Activity to an Event later?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade an activity to an event if attendance grows.

**Q: How do I manage attendees?**
A: View attendee list, send messages, check in attendees (for events), manage tickets.

**Q: What if someone doesn't show up?**
A: You can mark them as "no-show" in the system. For paid activities, you may keep payment (check terms).

**Q: Can I create private activities?**
A: Yes, select "Private" visibility. Only invited users can see and join.

### Payment Questions

**Q: How much do I pay in fees?**
A: 4% processing fee applies to all transactions. You receive 96% of the payment.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Payouts are typically weekly. Check your monetization dashboard for schedule.

**Q: What payment methods do students use?**
A: Credit/debit cards and cash (for onsite classes). Platform handles all processing.

**Q: Can I offer discounts?**
A: Yes, create discount codes or offer early bird pricing for events.

### Technical Questions

**Q: How do I upload images?**
A: Tap/click the image upload area, select from device, or take a photo.

**Q: Can I schedule classes in advance?**
A: Yes, you can create classes weeks or months in advance.

**Q: How do I cancel a class/activity?**
A: Go to the detail page, tap "Cancel", confirm cancellation. Attendees will be notified.

**Q: What if I need to reschedule?**
A: Edit the date/time. All attendees will be notified of the change.
