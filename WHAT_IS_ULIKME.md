## What is Ulikme?

Ulikme is a platform that helps people **learn, connect, and build communities** through real-world experiences and online classes.

Think of it as a combination of:
- **LinkedIn** - Professional networking and connections
- **Instagram** - Social features, stories, following people
- **Meetup** - Finding and joining local activities
- **MasterClass** - Learning from experts and influencers
- **Facebook Groups** - Building communities around interests

But all in one place, designed for **real connections** and **meaningful experiences**.
